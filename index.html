Galacticdef — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026

The short version

Galacticdef does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The game is fully offline and requires no account, login, or sign-up to play.

Information we collect

None. Galacticdef does not ask for, access, or gather any personal information such as your name, email address, contacts, location, photos, microphone, camera, or device identifiers.

Data stored on your device

The game saves your progress — completed levels, earned stars, and best-wave records — locally on your device using Apple's standard on-device storage (UserDefaults). This information never leaves your device, is not transmitted to us or any third party, and is removed if you delete the app.

Network use

Galacticdef makes no network requests. It works entirely without an internet connection and sends no data to any server.

Analytics, ads, and tracking

The app contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking software of any kind. We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies.

Third-party services

Galacticdef does not integrate any third-party SDKs or services that collect data.

Children's privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
